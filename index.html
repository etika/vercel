Questions

Frequently asked questions

Common questions from founders before we start working together.

How much does it cost to build a SaaS MVP with Ahuja Labs?

Cost depends on scope, but most SaaS MVPs on Ruby on Rails start with a 4–8 week build. We'll scope your specific requirements on a free consultation call and give you a fixed estimate before any work begins.

Do you work with early-stage startups or only established companies?

Both. We've built greenfield MVPs for pre-seed founders and optimized production systems for scale-ups like Builder.ai and Zencargo. If you need a technical partner rather than a full internal team, we're a fit.

What AI/LLM providers do you integrate?

We work with OpenAI and Anthropic's Claude models most often, and build RAG pipelines, WhatsApp AI agents, and custom automation on top of either — whichever fits your product and budget.

How fast can you turn around an MVP?

A focused SaaS MVP typically takes 4–8 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on scope. We'll give you a realistic timeline on the first call, not an inflated one to win the project.

Do I work directly with the engineer, or through account managers?

Directly with the engineer. Ahuja Labs is a boutique studio — no junior handoffs, no account-manager layer between you and the person writing your code.
